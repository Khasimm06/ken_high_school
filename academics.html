Academic Structure by Levels

Pre-Primary (Nursery, LKG, UKG)

Objective: Develop social, emotional, motor, and early cognitive skills.

  • Language development, storytelling, rhymes
  • Fine motor skills, hand-eye coordination
  • Basic numeracy (1-20, shapes, colors)
  • Social skills, hygiene, play-based learning

Primary (Class 1-5)

Objective: Build foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and values.

  • English, Telugu/Hindi, Math, EVS, Computers
  • Reading, writing fluency & basic math operations
  • Activity-based learning and term assessments

Middle School (Class 6-8)

Objective: Deepen conceptual understanding and skill-building.

  • English, Math, Science, Social, Computers
  • Project-based learning, logical reasoning
  • Quizzes, exhibitions, co-curricular growth

Secondary (Class 9-10)

Objective: Board exam readiness and career foundation.

  • English, Math, Science, Social, Second Language
  • Lab work, pre-finals, extra coaching
  • Career awareness and guidance sessions
