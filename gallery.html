Independence Day

Celebrated on 15th August every year, is a proud and significant occasion for all Indians. It marks the day in 1947 when India gained freedom from British colonial rule. In this day is celebrated with great enthusiasm and patriotic fervor.

The day usually begins with the hoisting of the national flag by the school principal or a chief guest, followed by the singing of the national anthem, "Jana Gana Mana." Students and teachers alike stand in attention, honoring the sacrifices made by our freedom fighters. Speeches are delivered by our students and faculty, highlighting the importance of freedom, the struggles faced during the independence movement, and the responsibilities of citizens in a free India.

Cultural programs are an integral part of the celebration. Students participate in patriotic songs, dances, skits, and poetry recitations that reflect India's journey towards independence. Many schools also organize competitions such as essay writing, drawing, and elocution to engage students creatively with the theme of independence. Teachers take this opportunity to instill values such as unity, discipline, and national pride in students. Decorations in the school are usually vibrant, with tri-color balloons, flags, and rangoli adorning the campus.

Annual Sports Day

Sports Day at KEN E.M High School typically begins with a grand opening ceremony featuring a march-past by all houses, the lighting of the sports flame, and an oath of fair play. The school playground transforms into a colorful arena filled with cheering, banners, and excitement. Students from various classes participate in a range of athletic events including sprints, long jump, relay races, sack races, and tug of war. There are also fun activities and games for teachers and parents, making it a community celebration.

The event aims to promote physical fitness, teamwork, discipline, and leadership skills among students. Winners are awarded medals and certificates, and special trophies are given to the best-performing houses.The principal and chief guests deliver motivational speeches highlighting the importance of sports in shaping character and building resilience. Sports Day at KEN E.M High School not only celebrates talent and hard work but also reinforces the values of fair competition and mutual respect among all.
